Introduction to Membership

Join the Budva Chamber, the largest business association of the municipality of Budva, and strengthen your business and local community. Membership in the Budva Chamber is a business involvement, and like all investments, a good business owner expects positive results. This is why Budva Chamber provides a range of benefits designed to increase the profitability of your business and build a better community. The Budva Chamber of Commerce offers four levels of membership per year, each suitable for different types, sizes and circumstances of business in Budva municipality.
