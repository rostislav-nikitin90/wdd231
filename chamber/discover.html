Discover Budva

City Facts

Location: Adriatic coast in Montenegro

History: one of the oldest settlements on the Adriatic coast, with a history dating back 2,500 years

Tourism: known for its well-preserved medieval walled city, sandy beaches, and vibrant nightlife as a major tourist destination in Montenegro

Events: various cultural events throughout the year, including music festivals, theater performances, and art exhibitions

Diverse Influences: influenced by various cultures over the centuries, including Roman, Byzantine, Venetian, and Ottoman

Demographics

Population: approximately 19,2181 (as of 2023)

Municipality Population: around 27,4452 (includes neighboring towns like Bečići and Petrovac)

Statistics

Area: town and municipality - 4.2 km² (1.6 sq mi)

Elevation: 3 meters (10 feet) above sea level

Climate: Mediterranean climate (Csa), characterized by hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters

Tourist Visits: over 700,000 tourists annually

Economy: tourism as the primary economic activity, contributing significantly to the local economy

Transport: Tivat Airport - 20 km (12 mi) away
